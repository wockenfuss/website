This film has no title (2001)

16mm film

90 minutes

“This 90-minute feature that Coughenour prefers to leave untitled is shockingly inventive. Unfolding at a pace more mindful than cineplexic, it chronicles the psyches and everyday lives of three denizens of the suburban netherworld, that nowheresville that seems to sprawl endlessly around us. Stunning moments follow one upon another as this black-and-white dream of reality takes us through states of light (and darkness) we’ve never known—a truly original, poetic work announcing a major talent.” George Tysh, The Metro Times
