Exercises in live composition exploring the emotional interplay between abstract and concrete which, through the application of cold mathematical analysis and synthesis grafted into the warmth of associative representational imagery, within the collision of technological and emotional manipulation, seek the elusive algorithm of sentiment.

These works employ a meticulously selected sampling of both stuffily old-fashioned and trendily newfangled strategies culled from the history of audio and video synthesis including--but not limited to--computer vision, phase vocoding, FFT analysis, granular synthesis, filtration, gesture-mapping, and stochastic algorithms.

The Indomitable Human Spirit (2008)

Approx. 25 minutes

A live audio-visual performance for thumb piano, Guitar Hero video game controller, and computer which incorporates a broad array of audio and video analysis and synthesis strategies to create a hinted and tenuous relationship between sounds and images, between abstract and concrete, between performance gesture and software response.

The visuals for this project are generated with Processing; the audio with PureData; communication between them via Open Sound Control.
